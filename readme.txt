This plugin adds UK address postcode lookup functionality on the checkout page of your website and is exclusively designed for WooCommerce.

== Description ==

Our plugin allows your website visitors to experience a faster checkout by simply entering their postcode and selecting their address from a dropwdown list.

= Address postcode lookup =

Adds an address dropdown list upon postcode entry on your WooCommerce checkout page.

AutoPostcode helps your users to experience a smoother and faster checkout by simply entering their postcode and selecting their address. This can help to improve your website conversion rate, reduce abandoned cart and also minimise shipping errors.

= Improve Conversion Rates =
The faster it is for a user to checkout, the more conversions you can receive. This plugin speeds your checkout.

= Reduce Shipping Errors =
By enabling the users to select an address from a pre-defined address list reduces shipping errors as there is minimal chance of typos and incorrectly entered addresses.

= How to Use =
Simply create an account on app.autopostcode.com and generate your secret key. Enter the key into your Auto Postcode Plugin settings. That's it.
